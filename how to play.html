How to Create Team

You can only pick a maximum of 10 players from one team. Your fantasy cricket team needs to constitute the following:

Wicket-Keepers (Minimum - 1, Maximum - 8)

Batsmen (Minimum - 1, Maximum - 8)

All-rounders (Minimum - 1, Maximum - 8)

Bowlers (Minimum - 1, Maximum - 8)

Player Combinations

On selecting the 11 players, you will need to assign a captain and vice-captain. The captain will give you 2x points scored by them in the actual match. The vice-captain will give you 1.5x points scored by them in the actual match.

Once you have chosen your combination, save your team, and you are good to go. Starting points are assigned to any player based on the announcement of his/her inclusion in the team. You should pick players that you feel will earn maximum points on the given day.
