LEGALITIES

GAME OF SKILLS

BigFan11 is considered a “Game of Skill.” The game of skills can be defined as a game wherein the skills of the individuals participating in any online fantasy sports gaming play a dominant role rather than the mere luck of the individuals.

ALIGNMENT WITH THE INDIAN LAWS

The game of skills such as the online fantasy sports gaming are considered to be legal all over India, except certain states such as Sikkim, Telangana, Nagaland, and Andhra Pradesh.

SIKKIM, TELANGANA, NAGALAND, ANDHRA PRADESH, ASSAM, AND ODISHA (ORISSA)

BigFan11 does not permit the users identified as the residents of Sikkim, Telangana, Nagaland, Andhra Pradesh, Odisha (Orissa), and Assam to participate in the pay-to-play contest(s). You will not be able to withdraw if you are from the above-mentioned (Banned) state.
